Hello, my name is Maxim Pürstinger (WCA 2022PURS01) and I am the developer and owner of this website.


I developed this website with the goal of making commutator/algorirthm training for blindsolving more efficient, more effective and add lots of features along the way. This is my first programming project, I started this website on day 1 of my web development journey and learnt everything as I went.


It is important to me that everyone can use the website completely for free, therefore I decided to rely on contributions from the community to finance the website maintenance and development. You can contribute to the project´s development with a donation via the 'Donate' button in the top right corner. Any support is greatly appreciated and a massive help!


If you like the website and want to support the project for free, please recommend it to other cubers who are interested in blindsolving. You can also send feedback by clicking the 'Feedback' button in the footer. The feedback data will be used to improve the site.


If you would like to contact me, you can DM me on discord (@applesauce.com) or send an email to maxim.puerstinger@gmail.com.
